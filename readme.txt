The Comentarimo comment system replaces your WordPress comment system with your comments hosted and powered by comentarismo.com.

== Description ==

Take a tour of your brand's feed. Have an overview of people connected to it in different ways. Immerse in statistics and discover how engaging your content is.
Navigate through comments and likes, comment or share them. Interact with all users from one place. Filter through comments and users's profiles.
Look beyond the number of likes or comments you get on an ad campaign, product release, blog post, or video to understand how people are responding to it.

= Comentarismo for WordPress =

* Install on any website in a minute.
* Powerful moderation dashboard with spam detection, word filters, search and recommendation tool.
* Auto-sync (backup) of comments with Comentarismo database

= Comentarismo Features =

* Threaded comments and replies
* Notifications and reply by email
* Subscribe and RSS options
* Aggregated comments and social mentions
* Powerful moderation and admin tools
* Full spam filtering, blacklists and whitelists
* Connected with a large discussion community
* Increased exposure and readership

== Installation ==

1. Unpack archive to this archive to the 'wp-content/plugins/' directory inside
   of WordPress

  * Maintain the directory structure of the archive (all extracted files
    should exist in 'wp-content/plugins/comentarismo-wordpress-plugin/'

2. From your blog administration, click on Comments to change settings
   (WordPress 2.0 users can find the settings under Options > Comentarismo.)
